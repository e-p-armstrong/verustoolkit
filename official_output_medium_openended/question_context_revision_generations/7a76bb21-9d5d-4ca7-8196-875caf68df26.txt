    This pool can contain multiple currencies, and the value of the attached currency

    liquidity pools have adaptive supplies, meaning that the supply of the currency

    An **automated market maker** (AMM) is a system that provides liquidity to a market
    or currency. In Verus DeFi, the AMM is responsible for maintaining the liquidity
    pool and ensuring that it remains balanced. The AMM does not involve human traders;
    instead, it uses algorithms to manage the liquidity pool and facilitate trades.


    A **fractional currency** is a currency that is not fully backed by a underlying
    asset, but rather by a fraction of it. In Verus DeFi, fractional currencies are
    created by combining different currencies in a liquidity pool. The value of the
    fractional currency is determined by the weighted average of the underlying assets
