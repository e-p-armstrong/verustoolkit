VerusBox: Tools and Stats on Your Phone
VerusBox, released 23 December by community builders, is a handy tool for mobile users. Use it to see the blockchain and network status, such as hashrate and network distribution. View market cap and coin price. Use as a block explorer. And much more. A useful tool for every Verus enthusiast.
[Download for Android] [Soon for iOS]

VaultAlert: Check Your Vault Anywhere
Users can lock their funds in a VerusID with Vault. If Vault is activated, your funds are locked and can not be spent any more, while you are still able to stake. With the upcoming VaultAlert you can check the status of your Vault anywhere in the world. Just visit the website and search for your VerusID. It will tell you everything you need to know about your Vault. Vault status, type of lock, unlock blockheight and date estimates.
The first release of VaultAlert is estimated to be in January. Stay tuned! â°
